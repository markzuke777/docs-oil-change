What We Do

Under The Hood

Every car that pulls into the bay gets the same treatment: a thorough look, the right oil for the job, and a tech who explains what's actually going on.

01

Conventional Oil Change

The dependable standard. Fresh conventional oil, a new filter, and a check of every fluid under the hood — good for daily drivers with regular mileage.

02

Full Synthetic Change

Longer-lasting protection for engines that work harder or run hotter. Better viscosity stability, cleaner internals, longer intervals between visits.

03

High-Mileage Blend

Formulated with seal conditioners for engines past 75,000 miles — helps curb leaks, burn-off, and the wear that comes with a well-loved engine.

04

Multi-Point Inspection

Included with every visit — brakes, belts, hoses, wipers, lights, tire pressure, and battery health, all checked while you wait.

05

Filter & Fluid Top-Off

New oil filter every time, plus a top-off of washer fluid, coolant, brake fluid, and power steering fluid at no extra charge.

06

Fleet & Commercial

Standing service plans for local businesses running multiple vehicles — set intervals, priority scheduling, and one invoice a month.
